The technology we work on (and have fun with)

Oculus Rift

The Oculus Rift is a very capable virtual reality device. Like the HTC Vive, it is currently wired.

HTC Vive

The HTC Vive is the current king of virtual reality. The headset is wired, and we are working on making it wireless. When we need a break from that, we play a few rounds of Space Pirates.

Microsoft HoloLens

HoleLens is a very exciting augmented reality headset. Augmented Reality is different from virtual reality in that augmented reality adds virtual graphics to one's view of the real world.
