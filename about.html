ABOUT US

MISSION STATEMENT

The Virtual Reality Association aims to build a community of students at UW who enjoy virtual and augmented reality, while increasing interest in and access to these technologies across campus. The VRA provides a place for people to learn more about VR, work together on projects, and share their interest.

CAUSE

VR and AR connect people and are best experienced together. We believe VR and AR will transform our world and have applications from entertainment, to healthcare and education. Therefore, we want to empower students to be on the forefront of using and innovating these technologies. We hold weekly events including meetings, workshops and demos.
